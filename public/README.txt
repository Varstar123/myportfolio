PUBLIC ASSETS
=============

Drop these files here so the site can serve them:

1. resume.pdf   →  Your resume. The navbar "Resume" button and the hero
                   "Download Resume" button link to /resume.pdf.

                   "VN" initials avatar, open src/components/Hero.tsx and

                       <img src="/avatar.jpg" alt="Varun Narayan C"
                            class="h-full w-full rounded-full object-cover" />

You can delete this README.txt once the files are in place.
